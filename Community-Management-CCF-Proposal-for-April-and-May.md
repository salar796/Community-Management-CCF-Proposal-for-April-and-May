## 本提案的范围是什么？

本提案旨在为社区团队提供资金，以便其在四月和五月期间继续获得薪酬。这将为我们争取额外时间来确定合适的候选人，该候选人随后将负责提交必要的后续提案。

社区负责人已于 2 月 5 日公开宣布其辞去该职位的意图。在过去几周中，选拔流程一直在进行，但尚未成功。团队将启动新的选拔流程以填补该职位。

## 👥 什么是社区管理？

社区管理团队负责管理 Qubic 的 Discord 服务器和 Telegram 频道，并支持多个地区和多语言社区。社区管理在协议开发、治理与社区参与者之间起到稳定层的作用，同时也为生态系统中的其他工作组提供支持。核心团队由指定的版主和社区支持人员组成，同时也依赖志愿者，总人数超过 20 人。

**其职责包括：** <br/>🤝 欢迎并引导新成员 <br/>🛡️ 执行社区规则 <br/>💬 促进建设性讨论 <br/>🎓 提供内部培训 <br/>🔧 改善各工作组之间的问题解决

## 🔍 过去 1 个月资金的使用情况

由于市场环境和价格波动，社区团队采用了动态薪酬模型，以保持在分配预算范围内。尽管进行了这些调整，Discord 和 Telegram 的运营仍未中断，并始终专注于核心管理、治理支持和社区安全。

此外，由于 3 月价格上涨，团队从最近申请的预算中节省了 5550 美元。

在此期间的资金支持使社区管理团队能够在 Qubic 生态系统中持续维持一个安全、公平且活跃的环境。这包括持续的管理、社区互动、规则执行、对其他工作组的支持、多语言协助以及在 Discord 和 Telegram 上的文档维护。

## What is the scope of this proposal?
This proposal intends to provide funding for the Community Team to continue receiving their salaries during the month of April & May. This will provide an additional month to identify a suitable candidate, who will then be responsible for submitting the necessary follow-up proposals.

The Community Lead publicly announced his intention to step down from the role on February 5th. Over the past weeks, a selection process has been underway; however, it has not been successful. A new selection process, led by the team, will begin to fill the position. 

## 👥 What Is Community Management?
The Community Management Team moderates the Qubic Discord server and Telegram channels and supports several regional and multilingual hubs. Community Management acts as a stabilizing layer between protocol development, governance and community participants. It also brings support to other workgroups in the ecosystem. While the core team is composed of designated moderators and community support, it also relies on volunteers. Together they make up a team of more than 20 members.

**Their role include:**
<br/>🤝 Welcoming and onboarding new members
<br/>🛡️ Enforcing community rules
<br/>💬 Promoting constructive discussions
<br/>🎓 Providing internal training
<br/>🔧 Enhancing problem resolution among other workgroups

## 🔍 How We Used Past Funding during the last 1 months ## 
Due to market conditions and price fluctuations, the Community Team applied its dynamic compensation model to remain within the allocated budget. Despite these adjustments, operations on Discord and Telegram continued uninterrupted, with a full focus on core moderation, governance support, and community safety.

Additionally, it achieved a savings of $5550 from the most recently requested budget due to the price increase in March.

Funding during this period enabled the Community Management Team to maintain a safe, fair, and engaging environment across the Qubic ecosystem. This included continuous moderation, community engagement, policy enforcement, support for other workgroups, multilingual assistance and documentation across Discord and Telegram.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> We apply a **dynamic salary model** based on price tiers. A 3-level salary system, subject to modifications, adjusts compensation based on Qubic price. If the price drops below set thresholds, salaries are reduced accordingly. If the price rises again, salaries are restored to higher levels. This model aligns compensation with market performance. It helps manage budget during downturns and rewards during growth. This model has already been applied in practice and allows the Community Team to remain operational without requiring emergency budget revisions.
